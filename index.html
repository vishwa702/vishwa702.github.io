Hello, there!

Welcome to my little corner of the internet! I'm a Machine Learning Engineer & Researcher with a passion for quantum computing, AI, and all things data-driven. I'm motivated by AI problems that have a direct and measurable impact on the real world, and I love working on projects that push the boundaries of what's possible. I recently completed my MSc in Computing Science (Statistical Machine Learning) at the University of Alberta, where I explored machine learning, optimization, and quantum algorithms.

Beyond research, I thrive in team environments and enjoy leading projects that bring people together. As Chair of Q-SITE, Canada's first student-led quantum computing conference and hackathon, I helped organize a space for researchers, students, and industry experts to collaborate and innovate. Whether working in a team or leading one, I'm always excited to drive projects forward and make things happen.

When I'm not working on AI, you'll probably find me playing video games (currently addicted to Marvel Rivals), hitting the gym, jamming on my guitar or ukulele, or going Latin dancing. I love staying active, trying new things, and balancing my technical side with creative and social outlets.

This site is where I share my projects, research, and random tech adventures. Feel free to explore, check out my work, or reach out if you want to talk AI, quantum computing, or just nerd out over the latest tech trends.
